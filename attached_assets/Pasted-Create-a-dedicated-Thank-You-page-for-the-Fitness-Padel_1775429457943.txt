Create a dedicated Thank You page for the “Fitness & Padel Club” Recovery Pod landing page.

Goal:
This page appears immediately after a user submits the form on the Fitness & Padel Club landing page. The purpose of the Thank You page is to confirm the submission, reinforce the value proposition, and get the lead to book a call through an embedded HubSpot calendar.

Brand:
BH Labs / Recovery Pod

Audience:
Gym owners, fitness club operators, padel club owners, athletic facility managers, wellness-focused operators, and decision-makers evaluating new revenue streams and premium member experiences.

Tone:
Premium, polished, modern, high-converting, performance-focused, wellness-driven, clean luxury aesthetic.

Key message from original landing page:
- Turn underutilized club space into a high-value recovery revenue stream
- Enhance member experience with premium wellness and recovery services
- Recovery Pod helps fitness and padel clubs create a new monetization channel
- Zero complex operational burden with turnkey implementation
- Differentiate your club with a premium recovery offering

Build the page with these sections:

1. Hero Section
- Large headline: “Thank You — Your Revenue Projection Request Has Been Received”
- Subheadline: “You’re one step closer to turning unused club space into a premium recovery and wellness revenue stream.”
- Short confirmation text that BH Labs received their information and the next best step is to schedule a strategy call.

2. Booking Section
- Add a strong section headline like: “Book Your Strategy Call”
- Add short copy explaining that on the call, BH Labs will review their facility, revenue potential, space requirements, member use cases, and rollout options.
- Insert a clear placeholder for the HubSpot meeting embed:

3. Value Reinforcement Section
Use 3 to 4 visually clean cards with these benefit themes:
- New revenue from underused space
- Premium member recovery experience
- Increased retention and differentiation
- Turnkey implementation with training and support

4. What Happens On The Call
Include a short bullet section:
- Review your gym, club, or padel facility layout
- Estimate revenue opportunity
- Discuss equipment configuration
- Outline timeline and next steps

5. Trust / Credibility Section
Include a short paragraph referencing BH Labs’ experience:
- 5 successful wellness locations across Miami
- experience in high-volume commercial operations
- partnerships with EQUINOX and Reserve Padel Solemia
- equipment installed in medical facilities including Dr. Johnny Salomon in Miami

6. Final CTA Section
- Headline: “Secure Your Booking Time”
- Short text encouraging them to choose a time now while interest is high
- Secondary button anchor link that scrolls back to the embedded calendar:
  Button text: “Book My Call”

Design and UX requirements:
- Mobile responsive
- Visually consistent with a premium wellness / performance brand
- Strong spacing, clear hierarchy, modern typography
- Use subtle wellness/luxury styling with a clean athletic edge
- Keep it conversion-focused, not cluttered

Technical requirements:
- Build as a standalone thank-you page in Replit
- Include smooth scrolling for the “Book My Call” button
- Calendar section should be prominent above the fold or very near it
- Add a fallback text link area under the embed placeholder:
  “Having trouble viewing the calendar? Contact us at info@thebiohacklab.com”

Also include:
- SEO title: “Thank You | Fitness & Padel Club Recovery Pod | BH Labs”
- Meta description: “Thank you for requesting your Recovery Pod revenue projection. Book your strategy call with BH Labs.”
- Clean semantic HTML structure
- Modern CSS styling
- If using React, keep component structure clean and production-ready

Do not create a generic thank you page. Make it specifically aligned to fitness clubs, gyms, and padel operators evaluating premium recovery as a revenue and retention opportunity.
