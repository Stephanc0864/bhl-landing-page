Create a dedicated Thank You page for the “University Athletics” Recovery Pod landing page.

Goal:
This page appears immediately after a user submits the form on the University Athletics landing page. The purpose of the Thank You page is to confirm the submission, reinforce the value proposition, and get the lead to book a call through an embedded HubSpot calendar.

Brand:
BH Labs / Recovery Pod

Audience:
Athletic directors, university athletics administrators, performance directors, strength and conditioning staff, sports medicine leaders, and campus decision-makers evaluating recovery and wellness solutions for student-athletes.

Tone:
Premium, polished, modern, high-converting, performance-focused, credible, wellness-driven, clean elite-athletics aesthetic.

Key message from original landing page:
- Turn underutilized athletic or wellness space into a high-value recovery destination
- Elevate student-athlete recovery and performance support
- Recovery Pod helps universities add a premium recovery solution without major operational complexity
- Differentiate your athletic program with advanced wellness and recovery amenities
- Turnkey implementation with training and support

Build the page with these sections:

1. Hero Section
- Large headline: “Thank You — Your Revenue Projection Request Has Been Received”
- Subheadline: “You’re one step closer to transforming unused athletic space into a premium recovery and performance resource.”
- Short confirmation text that BH Labs received their information and the next best step is to schedule a strategy call.

2. Booking Section
- Add a strong section headline like: “Book Your Strategy Call”
- Add short copy explaining that on the call, BH Labs will review their athletic facility, recovery goals, space requirements, athlete use cases, and rollout options.
- Insert a clear placeholder for the HubSpot meeting embed:

3. Value Reinforcement Section
Use 3 to 4 visually clean cards with these benefit themes:
- Maximize underused athletic space
- Premium student-athlete recovery experience
- Competitive edge in athlete support and program positioning
- Turnkey implementation with training and support

4. What Happens On The Call
Include a short bullet section:
- Review your university athletics facility layout
- Evaluate recovery opportunity and use cases
- Discuss equipment configuration
- Outline timeline and next steps

5. Trust / Credibility Section
Include a short paragraph referencing BH Labs’ experience:
- 5 successful wellness locations across Miami

6. Final CTA Section
- Headline: “Secure Your Booking Time”
- Short text encouraging them to choose a time now while interest is high
- Secondary button anchor link that scrolls back to the embedded calendar:
  Button text: “Book My Call”

Design and UX requirements:
- Mobile responsive
- Visually consistent with a premium wellness / elite athletics brand
- Strong spacing, clear hierarchy, modern typography
- Use subtle wellness/luxury styling with a high-performance collegiate feel
- Keep it conversion-focused, not cluttered

Technical requirements:
- Build as a standalone thank-you page in Replit
- Include smooth scrolling for the “Book My Call” button
- Calendar section should be prominent above the fold or very near it
- Add a fallback text link area under the embed placeholder:
  “Having trouble viewing the calendar? Contact us at info@thebiohacklab.com”

- Modern CSS styling
